About

Guitar city started in 1962 from humble beginnings in a small corner of Templebar, Dublin. Our owner, John Doe came from a well known musical family. His love of music led to him starting his very own shop guitar shop and the shop has been the number one name in all things music since. An instant hit, local musicians would trust no one else but John and his staff for their professional expertise of all things guitar related.

In 1986, the store, now grown beyond it's humble premises, moved from Templebar to O Connell street where it stands to this day. From locals, to tourists, to professional rockstars, Guitar city has seen customers from all walks of life and continues to serve the musical community of Ireland since it's founding.

Whether it's an instrument, sheet music, or advice you need, our team at guitar city are happy to help with any of of your musical needs.
